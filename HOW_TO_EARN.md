# How to Earn Pitchforks

Learn all the ways you can earn pitchforks in the Devil2Devil economy.

---

## Earning Methods

### Daily Rewards

| Method | Pitchforks | Frequency | Limit |
|--------|------------|-----------|-------|
| `/daily` command | 85 | Once per day | 90 lifetime claims |

### Admin Reactions

Admins can react to your messages with special emojis to award points.

| Reaction | Pitchforks | Limit |
|----------|------------|-------|
| Daily engagement | 25 | Max 3 per user per day |
| Campus photo | 100 | One-time |
| Enrollment deposit | 500 | One-time |

### Role Bonuses

Automatically awarded when you receive certain Discord roles.

| Role | Pitchforks | Limit |
|------|------------|-------|
| Verification | 200 | One-time |
| Onboarding | 500 | One-time |

### Birthday

| Method | Pitchforks | Limit |
|--------|------------|-------|
| Setting your birthday (`/birthday`) | 50 | One-time |
| Birthday celebration (annual) | 100 | Once per year |

### Other

| Method | Pitchforks | Limit |
|--------|------------|-------|
| Fill out the email survey | 500 | One-time |
| Discord event participation | 25 | Per event |
| Server boost | 500 | One-time |

## Tips

- **Be active** - Engage regularly in the community to increase your chances of receiving admin reactions.
- **Use `/daily` every day** - Don't forget to claim your daily reward to maximize earnings.
- **Follow guidelines** - Make sure your content follows community guidelines to be eligible for rewards.
- **Check the leaderboard** - Use `/leaderboard` to see how you rank among other users.

> **Note:** If something looks off or you believe you missed a reward, contact a server admin.
