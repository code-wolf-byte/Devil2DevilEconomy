How to Earn Pitchforks

Discover all the ways you can earn pitchforks in our Devil2Devil economy system! From daily rewards to special achievements, there are plenty of opportunities to build your balance.

Daily & Regular Activities

Daily Reward

+85 Pitchforks

Use the /daily command in Discord once every 24 hours to claim your daily pitchforks. It's the most reliable way to build your balance!

Message Activity

Achievement Rewards

Stay active in Discord! Sending messages helps you unlock achievements that award pitchforks. The more you participate in conversations, the more you earn.

Voice Chat

Achievement Rewards

Join voice channels and hang out with the community! Time spent in voice chat counts toward voice activity achievements.

One-Time Bonuses

Verification Bonus

+200 Pitchforks

Get verified in the server to receive an automatic 200 pitchfork bonus! This happens once when you receive the verified role.

Onboarding Bonus

+500 Pitchforks

Complete the server onboarding process to earn a massive 500 pitchfork bonus! This is awarded when you receive specific onboarding roles.

Birthday Setup

+50 Pitchforks

Set your birthday using the /birthday command to earn 50 pitchforks! Plus, you'll get a special birthday announcement on your special day.

Admin-Approved Activities

These activities require admin approval via custom emoji reactions

Daily Engagement

+25 Pitchforks

Post meaningful content and get admin approval! When an admin reacts to your message with the daily engagement emoji, you earn 25 pitchforks. Available once every 20 hours.

Campus Pictures

+100 Pitchforks

Share photos of campus life! When you post images and an admin approves them with the campus photo emoji, you earn 100 pitchforks. No limit on how many you can earn!

Enrollment Deposit

+500 Pitchforks

Submit proof of your enrollment deposit and get admin approval for a huge 500 pitchfork bonus! This is a one-time reward per user.

400 Likes on FSD IG

Points Awarded

Help a FutureSunDevils Instagram post reach 400 likes! When you contribute to getting a single post to 400 likes and get admin approval, you earn pitchforks. Tag admins when the milestone is reached!

Achievement System

Unlock achievements by reaching various milestones! Each achievement awards pitchforks and gets announced in the general channel. Use /achievements to see your progress.

Message Milestones

Earn achievements for sending messages in the server. The more you chat, the more milestones you unlock!

Reaction Milestones

React to messages to show engagement and unlock reaction-based achievements.

Voice Activity

Spend time in voice channels to unlock voice activity achievements.

Special Achievements

Server boosting, community participation, and other special activities have their own achievements.

Useful Commands

/balance

Check your current pitchfork balance

/daily

Claim your daily 85 pitchforks

/achievements

View your unlocked achievements

/leaderboard

See the top pitchfork earners

/limits

View your earning limits and progress

Earning Limits & Theoretical Maximum

To maintain balance and prevent exploitation, some earning methods have limits

Daily Rewards

Limit: 90 claims maximum

Max Pitchforks: 7,650 (90 × 85)

Campus Photos

Limit: 5 approvals maximum

Max Pitchforks: 500 (5 × 100)

Daily Engagement

Limit: 365 approvals maximum

Max Pitchforks: 9,125 (365 × 25)

Activity Tracking

Limits: 50K messages, 25K reactions, 10K voice minutes

These limits affect achievement eligibility

Theoretical Maximum: 20,525 Pitchforks

Daily Rewards: 7,650 pitchforks

Daily Engagement: 9,125 pitchforks

Campus Photos: 500 pitchforks

One-Time Bonuses: 1,250 pitchforks

Achievements: ~2,000 pitchforks

Enrollment Deposit: Included above

Use /limits to track your progress toward these maximums

Item Pickup Information

When to Pick Up Your Items

All purchased items can be collected during the Devil2Devil in-person event during Welcome Week in the upcoming fall semester.

After purchasing items with your pitchforks, you'll receive confirmation that your items will be ready for pickup during the Welcome Week Devil2Devil event.

Event Location

Devil2Devil in-person event during ASU Welcome Week

When to Attend

Fall semester Welcome Week (exact dates will be announced)

What to Bring

Your student ID and Discord username for item verification

Need Help?

Contact admins in Discord if you have questions about your purchases

Pro Tips for Earning More Pitchforks

  • Claim your daily reward every day - it adds up quickly!
  • Stay active in chat and voice channels to unlock achievements
  • Share campus photos and engage meaningfully for admin approval
  • Complete your verification and onboarding for big one-time bonuses
  • Set your birthday to earn bonus pitchforks and get celebrated!
  • Remember: All purchased items can be collected during the Devil2Devil Welcome Week event!
